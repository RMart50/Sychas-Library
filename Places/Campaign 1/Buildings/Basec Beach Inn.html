Basec Beach Inn

The Basec Beach Inn is a small beachside tavern in Sachnoka that is built into the Henderbrick Mountains. From the restaurant portion of the inn, people can see into the mountain to get a view of Henderbrick City.

History

Wak Vak, Adon Knobro, and Jaster first enter the Inn with Sir Lannor after they escape a cult's underground dungeon. As they enter, Wak tries to hide his precense from someone he notices at the bar. During this time Jaster is also bothered by someone to meet them outside, which he does.
