Pecra Tenna

Pecra Tenna is a port city in the country of Naxgale that sits on the eastern edge of the ____ Gulf. The city is built in two sections, of sorts, with the land half mostly being where many establishments are. The port half is where most citizens live in their boats on the west side, with one dock left open in case a visiting boat stops in the city. The party first stops here on their way to Wak Vak's home town, Dislon.
