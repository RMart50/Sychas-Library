Henderbrick City

Henderbrick is a mainly dwarven city found at the edge of Henderbrick Mountains between the city of Julgust and the village of Sachnoka, in the country of Naxgale. It is a part of the country's capital district, along with Julgust, Sachnoka, and the Village of Oricron.
