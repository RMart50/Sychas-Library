Klang Metalbender

Klang Metalbender is a gnome who lives Sachnoka. The party met him at the Basec Beach Inn after escaping,and possibly destroying, a cult's underground dungeon built into a cliff of the Chaos Territory. He somehow knows Wak Vak, who has yet to explain how.

Klang is known to have a sister, with her own shop in Pecra Tenna, named Quench Metalbender.

History

Wak Vak notices Klang when the party first enters the Basec Beach Inn, but tries to hide from him. However, Adon Knobro causes a big scene while experimenting with his staff. This causes Klang to notice Wak. They have a short conversation, where Klang mentions that Wak could be in trouble with someone for losing something, then Klang leaves the group to talk about their upcoming plans.
