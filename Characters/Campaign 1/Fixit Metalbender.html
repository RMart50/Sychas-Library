Fixit Metalbender

Fixit (gov't name:Felicia) Metalbender is a gnome who lives at the Metalbender Compound, with her husband and daughters, just outside of Dislon. She is the wife of Mold, has daughter's Tinkerbit and Natalia, and is the sister-in-law of Mold's brother and sister, Klang and Quench Metalbender respectively. She was an inventor and blacksmith like all the members of her family. She is very nice and motherly to everyone and treats Tinkerbit's friend Wak Vak as if he is he is her own son.

History

Fixit was first met by Wak Vak and Jaster, as Wak wanted to go visit to see about getting a new weapon. She is very hospitable to the two, cooking them a meal and letting them stay the night at the metalbender compound.
