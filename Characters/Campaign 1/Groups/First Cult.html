Cult from the Beginning of the Game

A cult that Wak Vak, Adon Knobro, and Jaster were kidnapped by. They also kidnapped Sir Lannor and Daso Greenhill to be inprisoned with the player characters and, as it turned out, later sacrificed. Daso was revealed to be a member and was killed. Meanwhile, the cults leader escaped and the group has no idea about the cult other than they have somewhat of a relation to Adon's past, since he recognized the symbol on their mask.
