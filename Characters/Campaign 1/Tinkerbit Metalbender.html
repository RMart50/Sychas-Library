Tinkerbit Metalbender

Tinkerbit (gov't name:Taña) Metalbender is a gnome who lives at the Metalbender Compound, with her parents and sister, just outside of Dislon. She is the daughter of Fixit and Mold, has a little sister named Natalia, and is the niece of Mold's brother and sister, Klang and Quench Metalbender respectively. She is an inventor and blacksmith like all the members of her family. She has a close relationship to Wak Vak, even letting him test her inventions. She tends to curse when she struggles with a project she is working on.

History

Tinkerbit was first met by Wak and Jaster, as Wak Vak goes to the Metalbender compound to tell her about losing her prototype, ask for another one, and ask her father about placing a ruby in a kitchen knife he stole. Tinkerbit gets very mad about Wak losing her prototype, but does agree to make a new one for him to test out. She explains that it will take some time and will find a way to send the new prototype to Wak. Jaster then asks her, in secret, to help polish his armor. She agrees to do it for a price, where she considers the fact that most people are offended by the fact he still wears and cares for his "ancient draconian ancestrial" armor, something her aunt did not agree to do.
