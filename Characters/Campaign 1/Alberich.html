Alberich

Alberich is one of the nine rulers of Henderbrick City, along with his twin sister. He hires the party to help him with what he believes is a growing goblin problem in the city. He is, at most times, accompanied by his assassin, Kada, and personal guard, Grakilm.

History

The Party (well Adon, Wak, and Jaster; they hadn't met Baelon yet) first meets Alberich after they are arrested in Henderbrick City for a misunderstanding from fighting goblins. Alberich argued with his sister during the trial that the party has helped them, with an older ruler backing him up. Alberich shows concern that the goblins appearing is a part of a bigger problem happenning in the back of the mines. As his sister will not allow any guards to go check on his "superstitions", Alberich offers to pay the Party 7 Gold to go with him to check instead. The rest of the rulers agreed to this, and Wak convinced his two companions to take the job for the money. He lets the Party stay in a guest suite of the rulers building.

The next morning, the Party joined Alberich and his two guards, Kada and Grakilm, for talking to the managers of the mine about the problem and making a plan. They decide to journey past the mine to the rest of the cave. It is during this journey that they find Baelon has been living there. With not much convincing, Baelon joined their troop.

The group, along with their new member, then proceeded farther down the cave. They then came upon an unknown camp, and sent Wak Vak to check out. As he finds the camp has two Duergar, Dark Dwarves, in command of a few goblins. However, it is also at this point that the camp notices the glow of Adon down the cave and go to attack the rest of the group. During this fight, Alberich struggled actually hitting his opponents, but did not back down and had some help from Grakilm to keep him from dying.

As the last Duergar was killed, he mentions how more are on the way. Alberich tells the Party to take an injured Kada with them back to the city to warn of the incoming danger, while Grakilm and him stay back to slow them down. Wak convinced the rest of the Partyto stay behind and let Kada go by herself, of course to try and make more money from the job. The Party came up with a plan only to find out they greatly underestimated how much reinforcements were on the way, an entire army. The Party then decides to run away as fast as they can back to the city while trying to keep their cash cow, I mean Alberich, safe. They eventually run into the Army of Henderbrick City ready for war with the Party's persuers. Once they reach safety, Alberich has Grakilm take the Party out of the city and pay them while he stays to keep fighting.
