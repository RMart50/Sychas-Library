Jaster

Jaster is the player character of Andrew Torrez and his "Greartword", if you know what I mean ;). He is a white/silver scaled Dragonborn uses cold breath to freeze his enemies. He also has the ability to heal his allies.

Jaster has shown to be horny at times, but his awkwardness seems to get in the way. He wears a cloak, that he started wearing in Sachnoka, over his armor.
