Mold Metalbender

Mold (gov't name:Manolo) Metalbender is a gnome who lives at the Metalbender Compound, with his wife and daughters, just outside of Dislon. He is the husband of Fixit, has daughter's Tinkerbit and Natalia, and is the brother of Klang and Quench Metalbender. He is an inventor and blacksmith like all the members of his family.

History

Mold was first met by Wak Vak and Jaster, as Wak wanted to go visit Tinkerbit about a new weaopon. Mold was busy working on something the two did not ask about. Wak asked Mold if he could put a ruby he stole from the Henderbrick mines into a kitchen knife he also stole from the Henderbrick City guest quarters. Mold agrees and works on the knife overnight.
