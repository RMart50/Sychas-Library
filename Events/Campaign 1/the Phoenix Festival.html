Phoenix Festival

The Phoenix Festival is about a month long event that takes place in the Pool of Phoenix Ashes in the desert in the east of Naxgale. It is a festival in only name, as many people just show up randomly during this time. Although, many people can be there at a time if they show up before the Phoenixes. The reason being that the festival revolves around people going to watch the Phoenixes death and rebirth that happens once a year and/or collecting the ashes that are left after they leave.
